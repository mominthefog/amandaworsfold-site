# Amanda Worsfold — Target Audience & ICP

## Primary Audiences

### 1. Solopreneurs
**Profile:** Independent professionals running their own practice or business.

**Pain Points:**
- Want AI leverage but don't have time to figure it out
- Drowning in scattered docs
- Answering the same questions repeatedly
- Recreating work already done
- Can't take a vacation because everything is in their head

**What They Need:** Systems that give them back time and make their knowledge accessible.

---

### 2. Small Business Owners
**Profile:** Owners of established small businesses looking to scale efficiency.

**Pain Points:**
- Know AI can help but need someone who speaks business, not just tech
- Can't afford a full-time AI hire yet
- Knowledge is scattered and hard to access
- Want AI that sounds like them, not like a robot

**What They Need:** Practical AI implementation from someone who understands business context.

---

### 3. Founders & Executives
**Profile:** Startup founders, C-suite executives, senior leaders.

**Pain Points:**
- Moving fast and need AI that keeps up—without the learning curve
- Spending hours on tasks AI should handle
- Need to show the board progress on AI
- Vendors are confusing everyone

**What They Need:** Strategic clarity combined with actual implementation capability.

---

### 4. Growing Teams
**Profile:** Companies in growth mode with expanding headcount.

**Pain Points:**
- Tired of being the bottleneck for knowledge and decisions
- Onboarding new people takes forever
- Knowledge trapped in founders' heads
- Can't scale the current way of working

**What They Need:** Systems that transfer knowledge and reduce dependency on key individuals.

---

### 5. Community Founders
**Profile:** Founders of membership communities, course creators, thought leaders.

**Pain Points:**
- Sitting on valuable content and IP that could work harder
- Valuable IP trapped in their head or buried in content
- Members have questions they answer repeatedly

**What They Need:** Ways to make their knowledge accessible at scale.

---

### 6. Fractional Teams / Companies Using Contractors
**Profile:** Businesses that rely heavily on part-time, fractional, or contract talent.

**Pain Points:**
- Need contractors and part-time talent to get up to speed fast
- Losing weeks to onboarding every new hire
- 5+ hours of calls to onboard each contractor

**What They Need:** Self-serve knowledge systems that reduce onboarding friction.

---

### 7. Trade Associations & Membership Organizations
**Profile:** Industry associations, professional organizations, member-based groups.

**Pain Points:**
- Member data, policies, and institutional knowledge scattered everywhere
- Staff turnover means knowledge loss
- Members ask the same questions repeatedly

**What They Need:** Centralized, queryable knowledge systems.

---

## Common Threads Across All ICPs

**Shared Characteristics:**
- Too busy running the business to organize the business
- Know there's "gold in the data" if they could just access it
- Have tried ChatGPT but it doesn't know their business
- Don't want generic AI—want AI that knows their context
- Value independence—don't want to create consultant dependency

**Shared Desires:**
- AI that actually works, not hype
- Someone who can translate between business and technical
- Systems they can own and maintain themselves
- Quick wins that prove value before bigger investments
- Hours back every week

---

## Anti-Personas (Not Ideal Clients)

- Large enterprises with internal AI teams (too much bureaucracy, different needs)
- People looking for the cheapest possible option (value-focused, not price-focused work)
- Those wanting someone to maintain systems long-term (the model is handoff, not dependency)
- "Just curious about AI" without a real business problem to solve

---

## Client Journey Touchpoints

| Stage | Experience |
|-------|------------|
| Awareness | LinkedIn content, Substack, referrals, speaking |
| Consideration | Website, services pages, The Knowledge Build detail page |
| Evaluation | Discovery call (Calendly 30-min) |
| Decision | Clear proposal with specific scope and pricing |
| Engagement | Hands-on collaboration, working sessions |
| Completion | Full handoff with documentation, no dependency |
| Advocacy | Results they share, referrals to similar clients |
