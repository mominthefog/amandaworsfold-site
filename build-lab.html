What You’ll Learn

See how to build it—then do it yourself

This isn’t about fancy technology. We built ours with a standard ChatGPT feature in about an hour. The magic is in the structure, not the software.

In this session, you’ll get:

  • The Cornerstone Documents Framework — the six living docs that turn a chatbot into a real team member
  • A live build from scratch — watch us set up a shared AI workspace in real time, step by step
  • A real user’s perspective — co-host Beth Lind shares what it’s actually like to work with a shared AI partner on a distributed team
  • Pitfalls and lessons learned — what tripped us up and how to avoid it on your team
  • Privacy and data security — a clear, empowering framework for what to put in and what to leave out

Everyone who registers gets a starter kit after the session: cornerstone document templates, sample custom instructions, and a platform comparison guide.

What We’ll Build

From real experience to your team

Amanda and Beth are both fractional on a distributed startup team—about 10 people, all working fractionally, different time zones, 5–7 hours a week each. They built a shared AI knowledge partner to keep everyone connected.

Beth will share what it’s actually like as the person on the team who uses it every day—from chasing document versions to having an AI partner that surfaces conflicts between decisions and connects dots across workstreams.
