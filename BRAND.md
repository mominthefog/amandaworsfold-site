## Typography

### Display / Headings
**Cormorant Garamond**
- Weights: 400, 500, 600, 400 italic
- Use: Headlines, titles, large display text
- CSS: `font-family: var(--font-display);`

### Body
**Montserrat**
- Weights: 400, 500, 600
- Use: Body copy, navigation, buttons
- CSS: `font-family: var(--font-body);`

## Usage Notes

### Primary Brand Colors
- **Gold** — Primary accent, buttons, links, highlights
- **Olive Dark** — Secondary accent, CTA backgrounds, hover states

### Color Pairings
- Olive dark background + white text + gold accents
- White/off-white background + gray text + gold accents
- Quote borders: olive-light (hover: olive-dark)

### Current Applications
- **CTA Section:** Olive dark background, white button, white/light gray text
- **Quote Boxes:** Olive-light left border (olive-dark on hover)
- **Footer:** Gray-900 background
- **Links:** Gold-dark (gold on hover)
